ABOUT

To cope with today’s digital hunger for new educational approaches. Yara designed Learn Up to make it easier to the parents to contribute to the development of their child educational level with an advanced and easy to use application that is designed to suit children of all ages and encourage them to learn more. Learn Up allows parents to monitor their children’s activities, and to choose what values they like their children to have by choosing the most suitable stories for them.
